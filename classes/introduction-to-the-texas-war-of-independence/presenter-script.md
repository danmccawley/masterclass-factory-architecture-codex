## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed analysis of internal divisions among Texans, including the perspectives of Tejanos who opposed the revolution, is limited and would benefit from more primary source evidence.
- Comprehensive coverage of the broader Mexican Federalist War context and how it influenced the Texas Revolution is insufficiently detailed in the current sources.
- More explicit discussion on the role and experiences of enslaved African Americans during the Texas Revolution is needed to fully address the social complexities.
- Some secondary sources lack extractable text or detailed content, limiting their immediate use for deep dives or nuanced explanations.
- There is a need for clearer explanation of the legal and cultural conflicts over slavery and citizenship rights that contributed to the war's outbreak.
