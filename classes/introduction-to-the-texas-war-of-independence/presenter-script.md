## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed analysis of Tejano perspectives and contributions during the Texas War of Independence is limited in the current sources.
- In-depth coverage of the Mexican government's internal political debates and military strategies during the conflict is insufficient.
- More primary source documents directly from Mexican officials or soldiers would enhance balance and depth.
- Statistical data on casualties, troop numbers, and demographic changes lack corroboration from multiple primary sources.
- Limited information on the social impact of the war on indigenous populations and enslaved people in Texas.
