## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed demographic and social composition of Texas settlers and Tejanos during the revolution period is insufficiently covered.
- In-depth analysis of Mexican government perspectives and military strategies during the conflict is limited.
- Comprehensive coverage of the role and experiences of enslaved people and indigenous groups during the Texas Revolution is lacking.
- More primary source documents beyond the Texas Declaration of Independence and official state materials would strengthen the knowledge base.
- Limited information on the international and U.S. political context influencing the Texas Revolution and its aftermath.
