## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed demographic statistics on the population composition of Texas settlers and Mexican residents during the period are missing or insufficiently corroborated.
- In-depth analysis of the perspectives and roles of Tejanos (Mexican Texans) and indigenous peoples during the revolution is limited in the current sources.
- Comprehensive coverage of the international diplomatic context, including the United States' role and reaction to Texas independence, is not fully addressed.
- More primary source documents beyond the Texas Declaration of Independence and constitution excerpts would strengthen the knowledge base.
- Some secondary sources lack extractable text, limiting direct evidence for certain claims.
