# Quiz Answer Key

## Question 1
What is the correct pattern to tighten wheel fasteners after mounting the spare tire?
Answer: Crisscross or star pattern
Why: Tightening lug nuts in a crisscross or star pattern ensures even pressure and proper wheel seating, preventing damage or loosening.
