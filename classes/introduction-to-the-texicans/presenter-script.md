## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed demographic and sociocultural data on the Texicans, including their daily life, family structures, and economic activities, are limited in the current sources.
- Perspectives of Native American tribes and Mexican citizens on the Texicans and their settlement are underrepresented, limiting understanding of intercultural dynamics.
- There is a lack of comprehensive primary source narratives directly from Texicans themselves, which would provide richer insight into their motivations and experiences.
- Some secondary sources, such as the Facebook post and Quizlet flashcards, lack extractable or detailed text, reducing their utility for evidence-based claims.
- More nuanced discussion on the transition from Texican to Texian to Texan identities, including political and social implications, would strengthen the historical narrative.
