## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed demographic and sociocultural data on Texican communities, including population size, family structures, and daily life practices, are insufficiently covered.
- Comprehensive primary source narratives specifically focused on Texican perspectives during key historical events are limited or not fully accessible in the current knowledge base.
- Nuanced discussion of Texican cultural traditions, language use, and interactions with Native American groups and Anglo settlers is lacking.
- Clear differentiation and historical evolution of the term 'Texican' versus 'Texan' and 'Texian' require further elaboration with corroborated sources.
- Some secondary sources, such as the literary work by Jinx Schwartz, may blend historical facts with fictional elements, necessitating careful source evaluation.
