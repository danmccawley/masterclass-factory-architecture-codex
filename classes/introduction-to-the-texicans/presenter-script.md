## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Limited direct textual content from some secondary sources such as the Facebook post and Quizlet flashcards restricts detailed evidence extraction.
- There is insufficient detailed demographic and lifestyle information about the daily lives, customs, and social structures of the Texicans beyond land settlement and political roles.
- More explicit primary source examples illustrating Texican perspectives, such as letters, diaries, or legal documents, are needed to deepen understanding of their cultural identity.
- Clarification and reconciliation of field disagreements regarding the precise definitions and usage of 'Texican,' 'Texian,' and 'Texan' over time would strengthen the curriculum.
- The role of women, indigenous peoples, and other minority groups within the Texican community and their interactions are underrepresented in the current knowledge base.
