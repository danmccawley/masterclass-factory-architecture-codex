## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed, focused primary source narratives specifically about the Texicans as a distinct group are limited; most primary sources cover broader Texas history without isolating Texican experiences.
- There is insufficient direct evidence or primary documentation on the daily lifestyle, social customs, and cultural practices unique to the Texicans beyond general frontier or Mexican-Texan contexts.
- Contested points or differing interpretations about the Texicans' identity, especially regarding their political loyalties and cultural affiliations during the Texas Revolution, lack multiple corroborating secondary sources.
- Limited information on the term 'Texican' origins in scholarly historical literature; more etymological and historiographical analysis is needed.
- The Facebook source (s6) lacks extractable text, reducing its usability for evidence or direct quotes.
- No comprehensive statistical data or demographic analysis specifically about the Texicans is available in the current knowledge base.
