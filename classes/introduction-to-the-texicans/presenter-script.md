## Research Gaps to Resolve
- Detailed demographic and socio-economic data on the Texican population during 1820-1836 is limited or not explicitly available in the current sources.
- First-person perspectives and cultural narratives from Native American groups interacting with Texicans are underrepresented.
- Comprehensive analysis of the cultural assimilation and identity transformation processes from Texican to Texian to Texan lacks depth.
- Quantitative data on settlement patterns, land distribution, and economic activities of Texicans is sparse.
- Limited direct textual content from some secondary sources (e.g., Facebook post, Quizlet flashcards) restricts their utility for detailed claims.
