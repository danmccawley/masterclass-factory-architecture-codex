- Limited direct textual content from some secondary sources (e.g., Facebook post and Quizlet flashcards) restricts extraction of detailed claims or statistics.
- Insufficient detailed demographic or sociocultural data on Texican daily life, customs, and internal community structures beyond land settlement and political affiliations.
- Lack of explicit discussion on the perspectives and roles of Texican women or minority groups within the Texican community.
- Minimal coverage of contested interpretations or historiographical debates regarding Texican identity and their role in Texas independence beyond noting field disagreement.
- Absence of detailed economic activities and lifestyle descriptions of Texicans apart from land grants and settlement incentives.
