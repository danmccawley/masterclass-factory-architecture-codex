# Quiz Answer Key

## Question 1
Which of the following best defines the term 'Texican'?
Answer: A settler in Mexican Texas during the early 1800s
Why: The term 'Texican' specifically refers to settlers in Mexican Texas during the early 19th century, distinguishing them from later 'Texians' and 'Texans'.
