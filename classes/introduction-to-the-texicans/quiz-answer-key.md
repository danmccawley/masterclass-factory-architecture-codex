# Quiz Answer Key

## Question 1
Which of the following best defines the term 'Texican'?
Answer: A historical group in Texas with Spanish, Mexican, and indigenous cultural influences
Why: The term 'Texican' historically refers to people in Texas with mixed Spanish, Mexican, and indigenous heritage, distinguishing them from Anglo settlers or later Texans.
