# Quiz Answer Key

## Question 1
Which of the following best defines the term 'Texicans' in the context of Texas history?
Answer: Spanish-speaking settlers of Texas before Anglo-American colonization
Why: The term 'Texicans' specifically refers to the Spanish-speaking settlers in Texas prior to and during early Anglo settlement, distinct from 'Texians' and 'Texans'.
