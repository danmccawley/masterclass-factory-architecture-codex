# Quiz Answer Key

## Question 1
Which of the following best describes the origin of the term 'Texican'?
Answer: A label for Mexican settlers in Texas during Mexican rule
Why: The term 'Texican' was historically used to describe Mexican settlers living in Texas during the Mexican Texas period (1820-1836).
