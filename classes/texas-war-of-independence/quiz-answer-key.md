# Quiz Answer Key

## Question 1
Which battle is considered the decisive victory that secured Texas independence?
Answer: Battle of San Jacinto
Why: The Battle of San Jacinto was the decisive battle where Texan forces defeated Santa Anna's army, effectively securing Texas independence.
